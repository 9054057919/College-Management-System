About Collage

Collages combine multiple photos to create a single image that tells a story or captures a specific theme. And collages come in many different formats, from a simple arrangement of a couple of pictures to a complex composition comprising hundreds of photos. You can use photo collages for various purposes, including creating unique home decor, memorializing a loved one or a special occasion, showcasing a portfolio or artwork, or simply organizing multiple photos into a single image for social media or personal use.
